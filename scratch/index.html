How to make a Pizookie

Here is a video to show you!



Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mix melted butter, sugars, egg, and vanilla
  3. Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt
  4. Fold in chocolate chips
    • Feel free to swap or add mix-ins
  5. Press dough into a greased 8-inch oven-safe skillet or dish
    • If using a different sized skillet, adjust baking time accordingly
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes until golden on the edges but soft in the center
  7. Top with vanilla ice cream and serve warm
    • Feel free to use any flavors of ice cream and various toppings
